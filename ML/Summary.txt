						 Summary of Blocks for Presentation

Data Sources

Sales, inventory, customer behavior, seasonal and trend data.

Provides the raw material for all AI models.

LangChain Orchestration

Connects all AI models into a unified workflow.

Ensures outputs from demand forecasting, segmentation, and campaign scheduling work together seamlessly.

Demand Forecasting (Time-Series Model)

Predicts future demand trends.

Helps reduce overstock/understock and aligns marketing campaigns with expected product demand.

Segmentation & Churn Prediction

Identifies high-value customer segments and predicts churn risk.

Enables targeted campaigns to improve retention and engagement.

Campaign Scheduling Recommender

Suggests optimal timing and sequencing of campaigns based on demand and inventory.

Improves campaign effectiveness and ROI.

Claude MCP

Summarizes AI outputs in natural language for the marketing team.

Makes insights accessible, actionable, and easy to communicate.

n8n Automation

Executes recommended campaigns, updates dashboards, triggers alerts to operations.

Reduces manual work and ensures operational alignment.

Marketing & Operations

Uses AI insights to deliver personalized campaigns, align inventory with demand, and engage customers effectively.

Feedback Loop

Campaign and inventory outcomes feed back into models for continuous improvement.

Ensures AI predictions and recommendations become more accurate over time.
